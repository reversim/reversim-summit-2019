Who We Are?

Outbrain is the world’s leading native advertising platform, guiding the digital discoveries of consumers around the globe. Genuinely connecting marketers, publishers, and the consumers in-between, Outbrain serves more than 308 billion recommendations, organically personalizing, and enhancing, the reader experience. Outbrain’s native advertising technology powers the news, product & information feeds of the world’s most recognized publishers and channels including MSN, CNN,BBC, The Washington Post, The Guardian, Spiegel Online, El País and Sky News. Outbrain is headquartered inNew York with offices in 15 cities worldwide the R&D center was established in Israel and with more than 200 developers.

Reversim & Us

Reversim podcast was established by Ori Lahav, Outbrain CoFounder, and CTO and Ran Tavory when he was a developer at Outbrain 10 years ago. The podcast gained listeners and followers and eventually became a proactive community that gets to meet once a year in Reversim Summit. Every year Outbrain gives the resources and takes under its responsibility all legal, finance and production issues of the summit. We are proud to take this part and be the main organization that stands and supports this activity year after year.
