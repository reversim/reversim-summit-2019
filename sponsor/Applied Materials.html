Who We Are?

Applied Materials is the leader in materials engineering solutions used to produce virtually every new chip and advanced display in the world. Our expertise in modifying materials at atomic levels and on an industrial scale enables customers to transform possibilities into reality. Applied Materials Israel leads the Process, Diagnostic and Control group of Applied Materials, combining multidisciplinary engineering group and top researchers in a wide area, from mechanics, optics and physics to image processing, machine-learning and material research. Our multi line of products enable semiconductors manufacturers to make sure their process is correct and stable and help them improve their yield.
